Recovery retreats

Mind successfully piloted Mind Recovery Retreats in South Australia following their successful launch in Victoria. These 3-4 day retreats for up to 8 people with mental ill-health provide relaxation, recreation, social interaction and new life skills. SA has nine more (COVIDSafe) retreats planned for 2020- 2021.

Measuring client outcomes

Mind clients can now track their recovery progress thanks to an app developed by Mind’s Research and Advocacy team and Digital Transformation team. This works by comparing a client’s self-assessment of various mental health and wellbeing indicators at the beginning and end of a support delivery. Mind is also using this data to achieve continual improvement across all its programs and services.

Resident at Brunswick West Supported Independent Living

My Better Life®

A new tool to help clients set their own recovery goals

My Better Life® is our conceptual model for assisting clients to identify life areas they wish to improve and to set recovery goals. Launched in the first months of 2020, My Better Life® gives clients their own plan by which they set their goals. Clients track their achievement of goals and satisfaction with their progress through regular reviews. Insights from the data My Better Life® plans provide will also support service design in the future

Improving mental health
and housing outcomes

How can you recover from mental distress when you don’t have somewhere to live that is safe, secure, appropriate and affordable?

In February 2020, Mind’s Research and Advocacy division released its ‘Trajectories’ research findings, in partnership with the Australian Housing & Urban Research Institute.

This important research, informed by interviews with over 130 carers and people with lived experience of mental ill-health, provides a body of evidence that unequivocally demonstrates the links between mental ill-health, homelessness and housing insecurity.

It will underpin future policy development and advocacy to improve housing and mental health systems to enable people to lead meaningful lives.
